### Option 1: Record New Video from Script

1. **Set up recording environment**
   - Clean desktop or terminal
   - Good lighting for screen recording
   - Microphone for clear audio

2. **Follow VIDEO_SCRIPT.md**
   - Execute commands in sequence
   - Show output for each step
   - Highlight key results

3. **Record narration separately**
   - Read from DIALOGUE
   - Record clean audio without background noise

   - Sync script scenes with narration

   - Professional title cards

## Recording Tips

### Audio Recording

- Use a quiet environment
- Speak clearly and at normal pace
- Avoid filler words (um, uh, like)
- Use professional microphone
- Record in lossless format (WAV, FLAC)
- Do multiple takes if needed
- Save best take as MP3 for video

### Screen Recording

- Use high resolution (1080p or 4K)
- Large terminal font for readability
- Dark theme for better visibility
- Smooth cursor movement
- Pause briefly after each major step
- Highlight important output sections

### Video Editing

- Use professional software (Adobe Premiere, Final Cut Pro, DaVinci Resolve)
- Sync audio with script scenes
- Add intro: "Anonymous Copyright Protection"
- Add outro: "Zama Bounty Track December 2025"
- Professional title cards for each section
- Smooth transitions between scenes
- Consistent audio levels
- Color grading for professional look
